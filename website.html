EV CHARGING STATIONS

Infrastructure requirements for EV charging stations

  1. A transformer and subsequent substation, as well as safety measures, must be installed.
  2. For metering/termination, a 33/11KV cable and supporting equipment should be supplied.
  3. Adequate civil works should be in place.
  4. There should be enough room for electric vehicles entry and egress as well as charging.
  5. Any local certifications that may be required. (This varies by state.)
